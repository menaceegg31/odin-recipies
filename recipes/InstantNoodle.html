Instant Noodles

Here is how to make a instant noodles

Required Materials:

  • 1 Microwaveable Cup Noodle
  • Microwave
  • Potable Water
  • Chopsticks or Fork

Instructions:

  1. Open the lid of the cup noodle while not taking the over entirly off
  2. Fill the cup with water up to the line slightly above the noodles
  3. Place the cup with water inside into the microwave and cook for 4 minutes
  4. Once finished, let cup sit for a minute before opening
  5. Open the lid and stur until the noodles are all free flowing
  6. Grab eating utensil and enjoy
