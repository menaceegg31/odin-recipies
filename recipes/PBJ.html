Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Here is how to make a peanut butter sandwich

Required Materials:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Butter Knife
  • Plate

Instructions:

  1. Obtain two slices of bread and place them as if they are the covers of a book
  2. Spread peanut butter on both facing sides of the bread
  3. Spread a heaping amount of jelly on one piece of bread, ensuring it will be enough for your liking
  4. Fold the side without jelly over to the side with the jelly
  5. Place on your plate and enjoy!
